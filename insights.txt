CUSTOMER CHURN ANALYSIS - KEY INSIGHTS

1. Overall churn rate is 26.58%, indicating that approximately 1 in 4 customers are leaving the service.

2. Contract type is a major driver of churn:
   - Month-to-month customers show highest churn (42.71%)
   - Two-year contracts show lowest churn (2.85%)

3. Payment method impacts customer retention:
   - Electronic check users have highest churn (45.29%)
   - Auto-payment methods significantly reduce churn

4. Revenue impact:
   - Estimated revenue loss due to churn is $2.86M

5. High-risk segment identified:
   - 994 customers belong to high-risk category
   - Characteristics: Month-to-month + Electronic check

BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS:

- Encourage long-term contracts through incentives
- Promote automated payment methods
- Target high-risk customers with retention strategies
